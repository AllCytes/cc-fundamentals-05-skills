Data Sources to Extract From

Vision Boards

Images, quotes, and aspirations collected over time reveal subconscious priorities.

  • Pinterest boards and saved images
  • Physical vision boards or journals
  • Saved quotes and inspiration

Task Managers

Historical task data shows what you actually worked on vs. what you planned.

  • Completed tasks and projects
  • Recurring patterns and themes
  • Abandoned or deferred items

Reflections and Journals

Written thoughts capture emotions and realizations in the moment.

  • Daily or weekly reflections
  • Gratitude logs and wins
  • Struggle documentation

Life Domain Categories

Organized areas of life reveal where energy and attention flow.

  • Work, Health, Relationships, Play
  • Monthly/quarterly goals by domain
  • Time allocation patterns

The 5 Slash Commands

/morning

Morning Agenda

Pulls yesterday's focus from Supabase, fetches TickTick tasks, categorizes by Eisenhower quadrant, confirms top 3 priorities.

8-10 AM | ~10 min

sonnet

/update

Afternoon Check-in

Recalls morning priorities, asks status on each (done/in-progress/blocked), updates TickTick, checks for new urgent items.

12-3 PM | ~5 min

haiku

/reflect

Evening Reflection

Captures 3 wins (Relationships, Personal, Health), scores productivity (0-100), generates tomorrow's priorities, writes to Supabase.

9 PM | ~15 min

opus

/eisenhower

Anytime Matrix

Quick view of all tasks categorized into Q1-Q4. No storage, just clarity. Use before meetings or mid-day decisions.

Anytime | ~2 min

haiku

/delegate

Automation Review

Identifies Q3 tasks (urgent but not important) that could be automated, delegated, or eliminated. Weekly review.

Weekly | ~5 min

sonnet

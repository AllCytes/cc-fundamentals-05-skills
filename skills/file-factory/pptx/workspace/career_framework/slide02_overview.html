What is the Career Clarity Framework?

The Problem

Many people feel stuck between multiple paths, overwhelmed by options, or unclear about their direction. Traditional goal-setting often fails because it starts with WHAT before understanding WHY.

The Solution

A systematic process that extracts insights from your existing data, applies root-cause analysis, and generates actionable documentation to guide your decisions.

Extract

Pull data from vision boards, task managers, journals, and past goals

Analyze

Apply the 5 Whys methodology to uncover root motivations

Synthesize

Identify patterns across life domains and priorities

Document

Generate Excel, PowerPoint, PDF outputs for clarity

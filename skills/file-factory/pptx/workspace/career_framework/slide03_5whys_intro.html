The 5 Whys Methodology

Root-Cause Analysis for Life Decisions

Originally developed by Toyota for manufacturing, the 5 Whys technique drills down to the root cause by asking "Why?" repeatedly.

Why It Works

Surface answers reveal symptoms. Deep answers reveal patterns. By the 5th "Why", you discover motivations you never consciously recognized.

The 5 Core Questions

1

Why do you feel overwhelmed or stuck right now?

Surfaces immediate pain points and emotional state. Reveals what is blocking forward movement.

2

What makes choosing between options difficult?

Exposes decision-making patterns, fear of loss, and competing values that create paralysis.

3

What is holding you back from committing to one path?

Identifies underlying fears, past experiences, or external pressures affecting commitment.

4

Why did you make your last major life change?

Reveals proven motivators and decision triggers. Shows what actually moves you to action.

5

What does your ideal life look like in detail?

Creates concrete vision across all domains. Moves from abstract desires to specific targets.

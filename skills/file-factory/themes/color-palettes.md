## Color Usage Guidelines

### Contrast and Accessibility
Always ensure sufficient contrast for text readability (WCAG AA standard: 4.5:1 minimum for normal text)

### The 60-30-10 Rule
- 60% dominant color (usually neutral)
- 30% secondary color
- 10% accent color

### Color Psychology by Category

**Cool Colors** (Ocean Depths, Arctic Frost, Tech Innovation):
- Convey trust, professionalism, calm, reliability

**Warm Colors** (Sunset Boulevard, Golden Hour, Desert Rose):
- Convey energy, warmth, creativity, passion

**Natural Colors** (Forest Canopy, Botanical Garden):
- Convey balance, nature, organic, growth

**Rich/Deep Colors** (Midnight Galaxy):
- Convey luxury, mystery, creativity, drama
